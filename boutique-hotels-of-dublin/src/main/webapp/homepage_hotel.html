Boutique Hotel in Dublin City Centre

Boutique Hotels of Dublin (BHD) was founded by Sean O Brien in 2014. Following a highly successful career in financial services in London, Sean returned to Ireland in 2012. Following the purchase of 3 premises from NAMA Sean established the BHD brand. Sean believes that a boutique hotel should be more akin to a restaurant than a traditional hotel.

Boutique Hotel in Dublin City Centre

Currently BHD is located in a court yard setting and consists of the Rose, Mary and Thyme boutique hotels. Restaurant services are provided by the Flying Colours restaurant which is located at the entrance to the courtyard.

In essence, the 5 star Boutique Hotel Dublin provides a rare destination where one can totally unwind and enjoy the comforts and service of a world-class retreat.
